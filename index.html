GQC

The GITAM Quiz Club is a unique platform to showcase your quizzing skills by assembling knowledge enthusiasts that engage in quizzing as a sport.

  • We intend to develop critical thinking, improve reaction time and help in building the IQ with the help of our extensive quizzes.
  • The club is extremely diverse and covers almost all the colleges of the three campuses.
  • Purpose: Uniting knowledge enthusiasts, as we foster a spirit of quizzing in the university.

GQC Community

Another distinctive feature of our club is the GQC Community where those who thrive in quizzing feel at home. They expand their knowledge by various means and would rather participate in quizzes than organize them.

Content Team

They are responsible for anything and everything relating to writing and editing such as

  • Writing contest for posters,
  • Posts on social media handles of the club,
  • Writing content for the emails which has to be sent to the students,
  • Drafting event reports and proofreading the questions made by the Research team.

Being in content makes you explore a creative way of thinking and enticing people by your words. So if this is a side you are familiar with then this is a place you will fot right in.

Research Team

They formulate and verify the questions that are used in the quizzes.

They are also responsible for preparing:

  • Questions for quizzes
  • Verifying answers
  • Looking or content to post on social media handles.

Quizmasters

They host the quiz competitions and tally scores to declare the winners.
They are also responsible for

  • Designing and moderating the quiz
  • Deciding the winners of the Quiz
  • Maintaining a record of attendance information.

Graphics Team

They consist of designers that focus on displaying the vision of the club via designing.

  • They design posters for quizzes
  • They also design posts and videos related to the quiz and handover to the Marketing team for promotions of the event

Marketing Team

They promote the club with the help of social media as well as handle liaisons with other organisations.

  • They declare the winners on our social media handles
  • Lookup up for collaborations with other clubs or SIGs

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is there a recruitment process?

    Nope, anyone interested is welcome to join. You guys can work with us for the next quiz. Also leads might get in touch with each of you before joining the main group, just to make sure you understand what the club is all about.

  • Is the club campus-specific?

    Nope, we've already got members from all 3 campuses. Also in the future, that is after college re-opens, we'll continue to work as one unit.

  • How does the club work?

    We are divided into 5 domains and every member gets to be a part of one domain of their choice for now.
    DOMAINS:

    • Content and Drafting team
    • QuizMasters
    • Research team
    • Graphics team
    • PR team
    Work is assigned based on the domain you belong to(more about domains will be discussed soon)

  • How is GQC working in the pandemic?

    We use Kahoot to make quizzes and communicate through the WhatsApp group chat, zoom calls, etc., and divide work among ourselves. Each domain has its WhatsApp group too so that it's easier to work with your group.
    Short term goals: conduct more quizzes have an active social media presence
    Long term goal: helping people prepare for and participate in regional/national level quizzes

  • Is GQC, a student club?

    No, Though we've already got 'club' in our name, we are still a SIG at square one and we've got some work to do to reach the student club status.

  • When will GQC become a proper student club?

    The decision will be taken by the director of student life depending on how well GQC works for the next 6 months/1 year.

  • What is the GQC Community Group?

    The community group is for people who are interested in quizzing in general and want to take part in quizzes. They can prepare for and spread the word about any out-of-college quizzes. If interested, community members can help with any sort of work in gqc and also take part in quizzes conducted by gqc. (Community members don't need to join any of the gqc verticals)
